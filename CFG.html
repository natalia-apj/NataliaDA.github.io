Code First Girls courses

Over the spring and summer 2023, I've attended a number of online courses run by Code First Girls. They run free coding courses starting from short MOOCs, 8-weeks Kickstarters as well as 16-week bootcamps. Their introductory to Tech sector courses cover a wide selection of different aspects of the coding world, from interactive websites, data and agile & scrum, product management.

My adventure and entering into technology started a bit earlier but CFG helped me to clarify the direction and definitely get more orientation about possible career paths. Some of the courses I attended include:
- Javascript (I received a "highly commended candidate" certification),
- Intro to interactive websites,
- Inftro to Cloud & Deployment
- DevOps,
- Agile & Scrum,
- Intro to Coding - Python,
- Machine Learning, team work with fantastic Ksenia - TEAM BLACK, GitHub
- Data & SQL. javascript Kickstarter course I attended which was a reward for personal involvement and fantastic team work.

During above courses I met and collaborated with different people. Some of those projects, though small, were hard, required huge personal involvement and massive learning. Nevertheless I met really amazing people and learnt responsible team work.
