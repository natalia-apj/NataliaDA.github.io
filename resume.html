About me

I am Natalia, a data analyst and natural science researcher passionate about working with organisations that want to use their data effectively. Through understanding companies' data I can support formulate initial ideas, what to measure and how to collect data. I will prepare and analyse your organisation's information and share my findings in meaningful presentation to advice you in evidence-driven decisions or share insights with new audiences. I also offer training in data manipulation.

I combine strong academic background, PhD in Biochemistry, with modern tools and technology to make situations better and impact the outcome. I am also interested in and available for freelance short-term projects and part-time longer-term engagements. I am a Data Analyst with an innate curiosity for the intricate connections between data, biology, and culture, backed by a strong scientific background.
